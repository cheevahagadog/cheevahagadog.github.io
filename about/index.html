About Me

Feb 21, 2018
1 min read

I’m Nate Cheever. I’m a pydealist (pythonista + idealist, yep I made that up). I believe that the right combination of data, visuals and narrative can enlighten, inspire and cause change. I’m passionate about making data products that help companies and individuals capture new insights and be more effective at their work. My favorite quote is “It’s easy to make a living; it’s difficult to make a difference.” This is why I love working in this field; I see data and data science as another opportunity to make a difference in the world.

I have worked as a data analyst, data engineer, and data scientist and prefer working in (you guessed it) Python for its flexibility and power. I currently serve as a data scientist at AdvancedMD. Occasionally, in my spare time I delve into data journalism and work in R and D3. I taught myself programming with R in college and attended Metis’ data science bootcamp while learning Python and data science.
